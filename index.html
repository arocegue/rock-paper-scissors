Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to Play

Choose your move by selecting a respective button choice and get ready to battle it out over 5 rounds. Each round follows the classic rules: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. Win more rounds than your opponent by the end of the match to claim victory. Once all 5 rounds are over, the final winner is announced, and a Restart button will appear so you can jump right back in for another showdown.
